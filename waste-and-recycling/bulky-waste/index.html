We offer a paid collection service for large household items that cannot go in your regular bins — such as sofas, mattresses, fridges and washing machines. Items are collected from the front of your property by a specialist crew.

Book a bulky waste collection

Prices

The cost depends on how many items you need collected. There are no discounts or exemptions available.

Number of items Price
1 to 3 items £62
4 to 6 items £124
7 to 9 items £186

Payment is taken when you book online.

What we collect

We collect a wide range of large household items, including:

Furniture and soft furnishings

  • Sofas and armchairs
  • Beds and mattresses
  • Wardrobes, dressers and tables
  • Chairs and cots

Large appliances

  • Fridges and freezers
  • Washing machines and dishwashers
  • Ovens and cookers

Electrical items

  • Televisions and computer monitors
  • Computers and audio equipment
  • Microwaves and vacuum cleaners

Garden items

  • Barbecues and lawnmowers
  • Dismantled garden furniture and swings
  • Large plastic toys

Where possible, items in good condition are passed to reuse shops or charity partners. Otherwise they are recycled or sent to energy recovery facilities.

What we don't collect

We cannot collect the following items through this service:

  • Bathroom suites (baths, toilets, sinks)
  • Boilers and central heating systems
  • Kitchen units and worktops
  • Windows, doors and frames
  • Flooring, carpets and tiles
  • Plasterboard and rubble
  • Sheds and fence panels
  • Asbestos — see how to dispose of asbestos
  • Garden waste — see garden waste collections
  • Plate glass and mirrors
  • Car tyres
  • Pianos

For items we cannot collect, you may be able to take them to a household recycling centre or arrange private disposal.

How to book

Book online and choose a collection date that suits you. You will receive a confirmation email with your booking details.

On collection day, place your items at the front of your property by 6am at your normal bin collection point. If access to your property requires a gate code or any other details, make sure to include this when booking.

The specialist bulky waste crew operates separately from your regular bin collection rounds, so collection times may vary.

Cancellations and changes

To receive a full refund, you must cancel at least 2 working days before your scheduled collection date. Cancellations made after this point may not be refunded.

If you need to change your collection date or the items on your booking, cancel your existing booking and make a new one.

To cancel, contact us using the details in your confirmation email.
