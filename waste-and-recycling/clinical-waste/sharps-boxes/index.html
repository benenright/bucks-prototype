We offer a free, confidential collection of used sharps boxes from your home. This service is for household medical waste only — it cannot be used by businesses or healthcare providers.

Book a sharps box collection

What we collect

We collect used sharps boxes containing:

  • Needles and syringes
  • Lancets
  • Needle clippers

Sharps must be stored in an approved, locked sharps box before collection. These are available on prescription from your GP, diabetes nurse or pharmacist. We cannot collect loose sharps or sharps stored in other containers such as bottles or tins.

If you also have bagged clinical waste to be collected — such as dressings, tubing or catheter bags — this is a separate service. Find out about clinical waste collections.

Preparing your sharps box

Before your collection, make sure your sharps box is:

  • Filled no higher than the marked fill line
  • Locked shut using the lid mechanism
  • Kept out of reach of children and pets until collection day

On collection day, place your box at the front of your property by 6am at your normal bin collection point. Our teams have until 6pm to complete collections, so please leave the box out for the full day.

How to book

Book your collection online. You will be asked for your address and to choose an available collection date.

If no dates are available when you book, all current slots are full. New slots are added every Wednesday and Friday, so check back then.

Cancellations and changes

To cancel or change your collection, contact us at least 48 hours before your scheduled date.

Call us on 0300 131 6000 and select option 6.

If we miss your collection

Our teams have until 6pm to complete all collections. If your sharps box has not been collected by 6pm on your scheduled day, you can report a missed collection online.

Leave your sharps box out until 6pm before reporting it as missed.
