Replace a lost, stolen or damaged bin

Contents

  1. What you can order
  2. What you cannot order
  3. After ordering

You can order a replacement bin if yours has been lost, stolen or damaged. Before you report a bin missing, check nearby streets and your neighbours' properties — bins are sometimes moved during or after collection.

If your bin was damaged by our waste crew, we may replace it at no charge. You'll need to provide photographic evidence when you submit your request.

Order a replacement bin

What you can order

You can request a replacement for any of the following:

  • General waste bin (grey)
  • Mixed recycling bin
  • Food waste caddy (23 litre, outdoor)
  • Paper and card bin
  • Garden waste bin — only if you have an active garden waste subscription

You can request the same size bin you previously had. Larger general waste bins (above the standard size) are only replaced if we have a record of a previous application. The maximum size available is 240 litres.

What you cannot order

Indoor food caddies cannot be replaced through this service. You can use any suitable container as an alternative, or purchase a new one from a retailer.

After ordering

Replacement bins are usually delivered within 10 working days.

If you have a damaged bin to dispose of, leave it at your property boundary on your next collection day and our crew will take it away. If your old bin has not been removed, you can submit a separate request for collection.

If you need to cancel your order, you can get a full refund as long as the bin has not yet been delivered.
