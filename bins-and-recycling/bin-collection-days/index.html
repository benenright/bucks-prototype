Check your collection dates

We collect four types of waste from most homes:

  • Food waste (weekly, in a small caddy)
  • Recycling — paper, card, cans, plastic and glass
  • General waste (fortnightly, grey bin)
  • Garden waste — if you have an active subscription

What goes in each bin

Putting the wrong things in your bins can contaminate an entire lorry load of recycling, so it's worth getting it right.

Recycling bin (blue or green lid)

Accepts paper, cardboard, plastic bottles and containers, glass bottles and jars, tins and cans. Items should be empty and rinsed but don't need to be spotless.

Food waste caddy (small, with green lid)

All cooked and uncooked food scraps, including meat, fish, dairy and vegetable peelings. Line with compostable liner bags or newspaper.

General waste bin (grey)

Anything that cannot be recycled or composted. Please bag your waste before placing it in the grey bin.

Collection changes

Collections may be moved if a bank holiday falls on or before your scheduled day. When this happens, your collection will take place one day later than usual.

You'll be notified of any changes through the My Bucks account service if you've set up alerts for your address.

Printable calendar

If you'd prefer a printed calendar, you can download a PDF version of your collection schedule. Enter your postcode using the tool above, then select View and download printable schedule.

Calendars show the full year and are updated every April.
